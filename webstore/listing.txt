Still from a Studio Ghibli movie as the Chrome new tab background!

This extension sets the Chrome new tab background to one of over 1,000 still shots from one of 27 Studio Ghibli movies. 

All images are retrieved from the Studio Ghibli website (https://www.ghibli.jp/).

The extension can be personalized by the options page (chrome://extensions/?options=fmdejajfcfdnbbanalgjibhcgbhihphd), accessed normally through the browser extension settings. The user-customizable features are: displaying or hiding the search bar, displaying or hiding the Google App Launcher, searching through browsing history, displaying quick-links to user's top sites, and opting-in or -out of stills from CGI films.
